# Coaching: transcript moments vs. audio clips

## Today

Syag does **not** retain meeting audio after processing. Speech-to-text runs on live or buffered audio; **key moments** in the Coaching tab are **transcript excerpts** with a jump-to control in the note transcript—not playable audio snippets.

This matches a privacy-first default: no long-lived recordings on disk unless the product explicitly adds that.

## Future: Work Coach–style audio highlights (opt-in)

To support short **replayable clips** (similar to some “work coach” products), a future version could:

1. **Opt-in** in Settings (“Save highlight clips” or per-note).
2. **Scope**: only user-marked moments or AI-suggested moments the user confirms—not full meeting retention.
3. **Storage**: encrypted local files or user-chosen folder; clear retention policy and delete controls.
4. **Permissions**: microphone / system audio policies already in place; document any new data handling in the privacy policy.

Until then, **transcript-grounded key moments** remain the supported pattern for evidence and review.
